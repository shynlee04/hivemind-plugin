   • Promote hierarchical access for AI agents and humans; expose full code trees excluding caches.  
   • Scripts should support discovery, exploration, and be reusable across environments.

6. **Tone & Language**  
   • Use logical, schematic reasoning; balance directive governance with patterns and anti-patterns; applying correct patterns to how-to-process skills vs how-to-implement skills

7. **Concision vs. Completeness**  
   • Be concise but never omit specificity or references.
