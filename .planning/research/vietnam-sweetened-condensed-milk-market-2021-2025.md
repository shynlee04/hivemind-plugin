## Executive Summary

Vietnam's sweetened condensed milk market represents a niche but culturally significant segment within the broader dairy industry. The market is heavily influenced by Vietnam's unique coffee culture (particularly Vietnamese iced coffee), where sweetened condensed milk is a primary ingredient. Key findings indicate:

- **Market Size (2025):** USD 33.85 million (TAM), projected to reach USD 49.21 million by 2034
- **CAGR (2026-2034):** 4.25%
- **Market Leader:** Vinamilk dominates with over 50% market share
- **Key Drivers:** Café culture expansion, urbanization, home baking trends
