# Phase 56 Contract: Trajectory & Session v3

Phase 56 is complete as a **planning contract**. It defines trajectory/session v3 scope without replacing existing journal, continuity, or delegation persistence.

## Boundary Contract

Trajectory is a truth-anchoring layer for checkpoints, evidence events, and recovery logs. Journal remains the append-only event timeline. Continuity remains durable runtime state. Delegation persistence remains dispatch/status truth.
