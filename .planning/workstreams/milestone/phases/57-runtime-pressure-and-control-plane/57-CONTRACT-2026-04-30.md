# Phase 57 Contract: Runtime Pressure & Control Plane

## Verdict

Phase 57 is complete as a **planning contract**. It defines pressure/control-plane decisions without implementing a new execution engine.

## Pressure Bands

| Band | Tiers | Meaning | Decision Pressure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Steady | 0-1 | Healthy or low-risk runtime state. | Allow. |
| Advisory | 2-4 | Degraded or cautionary state. | Allow with advice. |
| Gated | 5-7 | Risk requires explicit approval or deferral. | Require approval or defer. |
| Blocking | 8-9 | Unsafe or policy-breaking state. | Block. |

## Control-Plane Decision Contract

`detect()` must produce one of: `allow`, `advise`, `require_approval`, `defer`, or `block`. Each decision must include status, reason, severity, recommended action, and blocking rationale when applicable.
