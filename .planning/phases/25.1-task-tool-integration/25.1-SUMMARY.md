## Decisions Made

- Dynamic `await import()` for trajectory/contract modules to avoid circular dependency concerns
- Separate try/catch blocks for trajectory and contract creation — error in one doesn't prevent the other
- Deterministic IDs (`traj-${childSessionID}`, `awc-${childSessionID}`) for idempotency on retry
- L4_IMPLEMENTATION_TRACE as minimum evidence level (least strict) for auto-created contracts
- Helper method `createDelegationTrajectoryAndContract()` keeps `recordChildTaskDelegation()` readable
