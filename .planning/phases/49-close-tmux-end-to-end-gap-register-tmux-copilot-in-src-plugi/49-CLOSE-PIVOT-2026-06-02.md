## 8. Risk Acknowledgments

- **P51 carries the most risk**: rewriting the SessionManager 5 state sets in-tree without the fork's battle-tested race-condition handling. Mitigation: copy patterns verbatim (race condition, shell quoting, grid-planner debounce) into new in-tree files with explicit `// ORIGIN: opencode-tmux/src/session-manager.ts` annotations.
- **P52 risks consumer breakage**: rewiring tmux-copilot to the new in-tree classes could break the 12 existing tests. Mitigation: keep `TmuxCopilotResult` union contract identical; only swap the underlying factory.
- **P53 risks observer overflow**: 5s polling on many panes could overwhelm the journal. Mitigation: exponential backoff (5s → 30s after 1 min idle), journal entry cap (max 100 per session per hour).
- **P54 risks partial state**: detached sessions lose in-memory state across restarts. Mitigation: serialize session metadata to `.hivemind/state/tmux-sessions/` on every transition; reload on startup.
- **P55 risks over-promising**: 4 seed success criteria in one phase is ambitious. Mitigation: each criterion becomes its own BATS scenario; partial passes still advance ROADMAP.
