**Delegated terminal work now reaches parents through a shared summary-plus-metadata notification contract, persists failed deliveries in continuity, and replays them on parent resume without dropping the queue early.**

## Decisions Made
- Kept `transitionToTerminal()` as the single terminal owner and moved durability into the notification path rather than adding a parallel polling-based completion channel.
- Reused `notifyParentSession()` and `buildNotificationMessage()` for replay so immediate delivery and resumed delivery cannot drift.
- Allowed missing parent continuity records to be materialized lazily with a minimal continuity entry so durable notification fallback still has a canonical store.
