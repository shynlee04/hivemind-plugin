# Stage Model

| Stage | Primary Family | Entry Criteria | Exit Criteria | Success Metric | Failure Indicator | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Triage and contamination assessment | center | request is clearly detox/refactor oriented | one primary concern and family selected | no ambiguous family ownership | request still spans unrelated families with no partition | investigation report |
| 2. Context isolation | context + delegation | concern and authority surfaces are known | boundaries, constraints, and dispatch policy are explicit | no hidden authority assumptions remain | shared-state or authority confusion remains | partition plan |
| 3. History and memory retrieval | git-memory | continuity may affect recovery | history quality classified as usable, partial, or unsafe | atomic history assumptions and gaps are explicit | phantom resume/handoff routes remain implicit | continuity manifest |
| 4. Architectural decomposition | codemap | retrieval posture is known | rot is partitioned into bounded structural slices | slices map cleanly to families or seams | slices still overlap without seam ownership | detox assessment report |
| 5. Risk containment | system-debug + delegation | decomposition exists | rollback, quarantine, and safe sequencing are recorded | blast radius is bounded | unsafe edits would still cross unresolved seams | partition plan |
| 6. Systematic debugging | system-debug | unresolved behavior or breakage remains | evidence, containment, and rollback posture recorded | root cause or bounded unknown is documented | fixes proposed without reproduction/evidence | debug stage report |
| 7. Refactor strategy selection | center + codemap | evidence is stable | one reusable technique chosen per slice | technique choice matches defect class | refactor starts without strategy selection | refactor stage report |
| 8. Staged restoration | selected family owner | strategy chosen | bounded cleanup lands with verification gates | slice exits with explicit pass/fail evidence | family creep or unverified claims | refactor stage report |
| 9. Verification and regression control | context | restored slice exists | explicit verification and regression boundaries are recorded | pass/fail evidence is fresh and complete | success claimed without evidence | verification handoff |
| 10. Documentation and memory reintegration | git-memory | verified restoration evidence exists | retrieval/synthesis artifacts are emitted for future recovery | next-turn recovery is materially easier | changes land without durable memory artifacts | knowledge synthesis |
| 11. Follow-up stabilization | center + context | docs and memory artifacts exist | governance updates, follow-up actions, and monitoring gaps are recorded | next-turn recovery is deterministic | governance/docs drift still hidden | stabilization report |
