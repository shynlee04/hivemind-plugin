# Persona Matrix

## Vibecoder Signals
- Vague goals and asks for complete product quickly.
- Limited stack precision.
- Needs examples, tutoring, and confidence-building prompts.
- Prefers guided choices over open-ended architecture debates.

## Enterprise Signals
- Large, messy requirement dumps.
- Compliance, governance, audit, or scale constraints.
- Cross-domain dependencies and strict deliverable expectations.
- Demands deterministic outputs and validation evidence.

## Domain Signals
- Dev: API/framework/data model terminology.
- Marketing: campaign/content/channel/brand metrics.
- Finance: budget/forecast/reconciliation/compliance language.
- Office Ops: SOP/workflow/document policy and handoff controls.
