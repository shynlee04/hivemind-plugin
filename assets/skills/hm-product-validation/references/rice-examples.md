# RICE Examples

10 worked examples across product types.

## 1. New "Export to CSV" button
- Reach: 25% of users (Medium) — quarter
- Impact: 0.5 (Low) — saves 2 min per use
- Confidence: 80% (Medium)
- Effort: 1 person-week
- RICE: (25 × 0.5 × 0.8) / 1 = **10** — Build this quarter
